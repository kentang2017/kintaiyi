# 🌟 Kintaiyi 堅太乙 Wiki

Welcome to the **Kintaiyi 堅太乙** wiki — the most authentic open-source Taiyi Shenshu (太乙神數) divination engine in Python.

---

## What is Kintaiyi?

**太乙神數 (Taiyi Shenshu)** is one of the "Three Styles" (三式) of ancient Chinese divination, alongside Qi Men Dun Jia (奇門遁甲) and Da Liu Ren (大六壬). This millennia-old system was historically used to forecast celestial phenomena, national destiny, and major events.

**Kintaiyi 堅太乙** — meaning "steadfastly adhering to the ancient methods" — faithfully implements the classical algorithms without simplification or shortcuts, enabling researchers and enthusiasts to study, verify, and apply this ancient wisdom with modern tools.

---

## ✨ Key Features

- 🔮 **Six Calculation Modes** — Year, Month, Day, Hour, Minute, and Life divination
- 📜 **Four Ancient Methods** — Taiyi Orthodox (統宗), Gold Mirror (金鏡), Gold Panning Song (淘金歌), Taiyi Bureau (太乙局)
- 📊 **Rich Output** — Heavenly Stems & Earthly Branches, board formations, deities, 28 Mansions, 8 Gates, military predictions, historical reign cross-references
- 🏯 **Historical Reign Data** — Built-in complete Chinese dynastic chronology with lunar calendar conversion
- 🖥️ **Multiple Interfaces** — Python API, Typer CLI, and Streamlit GUI
- 🤖 **AI Integration** — Cerebras AI for automatic board interpretation
